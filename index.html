About Me

I'm Joel Wang, an expert in distributed systems, AI, blockchain, and cybersecurity. My journey spans from academic research to entrepreneurship, where I've built innovative solutions for secure and scalable technologies. My passion for technology fairness drives me to create impactful products that improve lives. I founded Nebulas and led the creation of PoD, and I'm now working on generative AI for legal tech.

Developed the PoD consensus algorithm, combining PBFT and game theory concepts. Nebulas handled 1 million transactions per day and attracted 2,300 developers globally.
